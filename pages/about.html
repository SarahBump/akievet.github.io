About Me

Born and raised in Wisconsin. Lover of pizza + ranch dressing. Graduate from the University of Wisconsin with a degree in International Studies - Environmental Focus. Business certificate. Spent a year in Copenhagen learning about post-modernism, Hegel, and Kirkegaard. Dabbled in Graphic Design. After one last six month adventure in Vietnam, I moved to New York and learned to code. Finally love what I get paid to do. Sometimes vegan, sometimes carnivore, most time somewhere in between. Weekend warrior, mountain goat, tech nerd. Very skilled at not being bored. Someday I hope to build a cabin in the woods with really fast internet and a small family of goats to keep me company.

Current hobbies include: hiking, swing dancing, bouldering, painting, electronics tinkering, running, bread baking, hammocking, slowly reading my way through Infinite Jest.
